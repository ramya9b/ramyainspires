Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

SU@Ramiya Inspires ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how we protect it. By using this website, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect what you choose to give us:

  • Name, email address, and (optionally) WhatsApp number — when you request our free prompts or ask to be notified about upcoming packs.
  • Order details — when you buy a product (handled by our payment partner).

We do not collect your payment card or bank details — those are entered directly on our secure payment provider's page.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the free prompts or products you request.
  • To reply to your questions and provide support.
  • To let you know when a pack you asked about is launched.
  • To improve our products and website.

We will never spam you, and you can ask us to stop contacting you at any time.

3. Payments

Payments are processed securely by third-party providers such as SuperProfile / Instamojo / Razorpay. They handle your payment information under their own privacy and security policies. We never see or store your full card or bank details.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Our payment and file-delivery providers, so your order can be completed.
  • Authorities, if required by law.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website stores a small note in your own browser (local storage) to remember if you have already subscribed, so you are not asked twice. Our site loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of serving those fonts. We do not run advertising trackers.

6. Data Retention & Your Rights

We keep your details only as long as needed to serve you. You may ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete the information we hold about you — just message us (details below) and we will act promptly.

7. Children

Our products are intended for adults and professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be on this page with the updated date above.
